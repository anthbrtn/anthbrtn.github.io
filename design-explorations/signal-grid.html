Abstract

I finished my Ph.D. in Communication at Simon Fraser University, where I was a Mellon-SFU Data Fluencies Fellow at the Digital Democracies Institute. My dissertation examines the relationship between social theory, intelligence, desire, and mimesis in contemporary late fascist politics.
