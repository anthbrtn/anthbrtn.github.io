Studies of networked publics, atmosphere, and recognition.

Burton researches how platforms shape authenticity, trust, and antagonism through interfaces, vernacular genres, and everyday styles of participation.

Current and recent work spans appification, reactionary media, misinformation, digital libertarianism, and collaborative projects with Beyond Verification, Open Intelligence Lab, and Infoscape.

Writing Authenticity, incel rationality, misinformation, rebel personalities. Labs Beyond Verification. Open Intelligence Lab. Digital Citizenship Cultures. Courses The Memeplex. Critical and Applied Data Studies. Methods and publics.
