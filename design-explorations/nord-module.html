Current work

Postdoctoral Researcher / University of Amsterdam

Research on platform atmospheres, authenticity, and political style.

Burton studies the media forms through which networked publics learn to authenticate themselves, stage affiliation, and identify enemies. His research ranges across appification, misinformation, reactionary media, digital libertarianism, and the aesthetics of participation.

Writing and teaching connect digital methods with close reading, visual culture, and critical social theory. Current and recent affiliations include Beyond Verification, Open Intelligence Lab, and Infoscape Research Lab.
