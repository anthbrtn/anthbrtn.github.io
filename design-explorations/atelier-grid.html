Research / Writing / Teaching

Authenticity, platforms, and the political styles of digital life.

Anthony Burton is a postdoctoral researcher and lecturer in the Media Studies department at the University of Amsterdam. His work moves between social theory, cultural analysis, and digital methods to study how platforms organize appearance, trust, antagonism, and ordinary participation.

Recent projects include writing on cross-platform misinformation and Canada's far-right media ecology, work on appification and digital libertarianism, and collaborations with Beyond Verification, Open Intelligence Lab, and the Digital Citizenship Cultures initiative.
