Research, writing, teaching

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Media Studies department at the University of Amsterdam. I finished my Ph.D. in Communication at Simon Fraser University, where I was a Mellon-SFU Data Fluencies Fellow at the Digital Democracies Institute. My dissertation is about the relationship between social theory, intelligence, desire, and mimesis in contemporary late fascist politics.

Current lines of work

  • Platform authentication and the social life of apps
  • Algorithmic authenticity, truth, and digital subjecthood
  • Political aesthetics, mimesis, and reactionary media cultures

Most recent book

Algorithmic Authenticity

A 2023 open access volume that asks how authenticity gets operationalized inside contemporary information disorders, platforms, and liberal-democratic truth claims.
