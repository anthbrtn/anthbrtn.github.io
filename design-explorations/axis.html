Anthony Burton / Media Studies / Amsterdam

About Publications Presentations CV

Research on authenticity, platform cultures, and political style.

Burton is a postdoctoral researcher and lecturer at the University of Amsterdam working across social theory, cultural analysis, and digital methods.

His writing examines appification, misinformation, reactionary media, digital libertarianism, and the aesthetics of participation.

Appearance, antagonism, verification, scenes of sincerity.
