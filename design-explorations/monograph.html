Anthony Burton

Internet culture researcher

I am a postdoctoral researcher in the Media Studies department at the University of Amsterdam. I finished my Ph.D. in Communication at Simon Fraser University, where I was a Mellon-SFU Data Fluencies Fellow at the Digital Democracies Institute.

My dissertation is about the relationship between social theory, intelligence, desire, and mimesis in contemporary late fascist politics.
