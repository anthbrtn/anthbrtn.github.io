Anthony Burton

Research into platform cultures, authenticity, and political aesthetics.

Postdoctoral researcher and lecturer in Media Studies at the University of Amsterdam.

About Publications Presentations CV Email

I finished my Ph.D. in Communication at Simon Fraser University, where I was a Mellon-SFU Data Fluencies Fellow at the Digital Democracies Institute. My work examines how desire, intelligence, mimesis, and subjectivity are organized through contemporary media systems.

Recent focal points include Algorithmic Authenticity, platform authentication, and reactionary digital cultures.
